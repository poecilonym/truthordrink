How to play!

Category:

You pick a category.
Example: Beer Brands
Then everyone says a beer brand
The person that repeats one twice or
does't know another brand has to drink

New Rule:

You make up a new rule
Every player that breaks the rule has to drink
Example: Tap your nose before you drink

Kings Cocktail:

Every time this card get's picked
you put another shot of liquid
into the Kings Cups
When the Cup is full the player that picks
the card again has to drink the whole cup

Hands Up:

Put your hands up
The last person to raise their hands
has to drink

Hands Down:

Put your hands on the table
The last person to lower their hands
has to drink

Hand to Face:

Tap your forehead
The last person to tap their forehead
has to drink

Drink Buddy

Choose your Drink Buddy
You have to drink together all the time
